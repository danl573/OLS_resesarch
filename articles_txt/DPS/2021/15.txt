dps traffic stop leads to cash seizure in maverick county (dps south texas region)

del rio – the texas department of public safety (dps) during operation lone star seized over $70,000 in cash after a texas highway patrol trooper stopped a vehicle in maverick county. on july 5, 2021, around 6:25 p.m., a dps trooper stopped a gray nissan passenger car traveling south on u.s. 57 near mile marker 396 for a traffic violation. during the traffic stop, the trooper discovered plastic-wrapped bundles containing u.s. currency in a hidden compartment in the front center console. the driver, 22-year-old luis fernando rodriguez of san antonio, and passenger, 18-year-old marc anthony rodriguez of san antonio, were both arrested, charged with money laundering and taken to the maverick county jail.  ### (dps south texas region) @txdps @txdps   txdeptpublicsafety texas dps mobile app phone: (512) 424-2080 email: media@dps.texas.gov general inquiries: visit the contact us page for divisions contact. open record requests: visit the public information request page.     © 2023 texas department of public safety. pdf files require adobe reader or compatible.
