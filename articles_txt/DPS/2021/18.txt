dps traffic stop leads to stash house (dps south texas region)

weslaco – the texas department of public safety (dps) disrupted a smuggling attempt after a texas highway patrol trooper stopped a vehicle in hidalgo county as part of operation lone star. on june 12, 2021, just after 11 a.m., a dps trooper attempted to stop a black volkswagen passenger car on 23rd street and u.s. 83 in mcallen for a traffic violation. the driver refused to stop, and a vehicle pursuit began, ending with the driver crashing into another vehicle on 10th street and jackson avenue in mcallen. through further investigation, troopers discovered the driver had left a possible stash house prior to the pursuit. troopers located the stash house and referred three undocumented males to u.s. border patrol. one of the men is reportedly a deported felon from a prior drug charge. the driver, 34-year-old, efrain eusebio barrientos, of brownsville, was arrested and charged with smuggling of persons and evading arrest.  ### (dps south texas region) @txdps @txdps   txdeptpublicsafety texas dps mobile app phone: (512) 424-2080 email: media@dps.texas.gov general inquiries: visit the contact us page for divisions contact. open record requests: visit the public information request page.     © 2023 texas department of public safety. pdf files require adobe reader or compatible.
