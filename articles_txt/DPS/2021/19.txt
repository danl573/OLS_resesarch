dps traffic stop leads to more than $100k cash seizure (dps south texas region)

weslaco – the texas department of public safety (dps) seized $147,490 in cash when a texas highway patrol trooper assigned to operation lone star stopped a vehicle in hidalgo county. on june 11, 2021, just after 7 p.m., a dps trooper stopped a gray toyota passenger car on ih-69c in edinburg for a traffic violation. a dps k-9 unit arrived on scene and alerted on the vehicle. the trooper then discovered a brown cardboard box and brown paper bag containing large sums of cash wrapped with rubber bands. the money originated from houston and was headed for mcallen. the driver, 29-year-old yeliena pedroso, was arrested, charged with money laundering and taken to the hidalgo county jail.  ### (dps south texas region) @txdps @txdps   txdeptpublicsafety texas dps mobile app phone: (512) 424-2080 email: media@dps.texas.gov general inquiries: visit the contact us page for divisions contact. open record requests: visit the public information request page.     © 2023 texas department of public safety. pdf files require adobe reader or compatible.
